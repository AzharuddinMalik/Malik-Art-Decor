P.O.P. Design & Installation

Elevate your interiors with exquisite Plaster of Paris designs. From intricate false ceilings to decorative wall panels, we bring your vision to life with precision and artistry.

  • False Ceilings (Plain & Designer)
  • Wall Putty & Finishing
  • Cornices & Mouldings
  • Custom POP Decorations

Gypsum Board Works

Modernize your spaces with versatile and durable gypsum board solutions. Ideal for partitions, ceilings, and elegant wall claddings that offer superior finish and insulation.

  • Gypsum False Ceilings
  • Drywall Partitions
  • Gypsum Wall Paneling
  • Moisture-Resistant Solutions

About MAD Associates

Your Trusted Partner in Interior Finishing

Founded over a decade ago, MAD Associates has grown to become a leading name in P.O.P. and Gypsum works. Our journey began with a commitment to craftsmanship, quality, and client satisfaction, values that remain at the core of everything we do.

We pride ourselves on transforming ordinary spaces into extraordinary interiors. Our team of skilled artisans and technicians combines traditional techniques with modern innovations to deliver durable, aesthetically pleasing, and customized solutions for every project.

From residential makeovers to large-scale commercial ventures, we approach each assignment with dedication, ensuring meticulous attention to detail and timely completion. Choose MAD Associates for reliability, expertise, and a flawless finish.
